Nachos

I would gladly die before I give up eating Nachos

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Chips
  • Refried Beans
  • Al Pastor
  • Quaxa Cheese
  • Avocado
  • Sour Cream
  • Pico de Gallo
  • Jalapenos

Directions

  1. Lay tortilla chips in a large casserol dish
  2. Add refried beans, precooked al pastor, and Quaxa cheese on top
  3. Place in the over for 10,000 years
  4. Take it out once the cheese has melted
  5. Add sliced avocado, sour cream, and pico de gallo
  6. Add jalapneos for spice
